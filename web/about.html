Hi! My name is Hernan Vogel, I am from Temuco (Chile). I'm just another Salesforce Developer.


I developed this humble app because one day, I needed to made a production deployment from multiple prior deployments to a sandbox (each deployment has a different package.xml). So I started unifying elements from around 40 package.xml files and I got quickly bored because of the slow, lazy and manual task of unifying everything on a single package. So I thought: what if I make a little app?


It's not much but I hope you like this app and find it useful. If you want access to the source code, visit me in GitHub.
